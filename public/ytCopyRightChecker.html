Copyright Tips

Standard YouTube License

All rights reserved. Cannot be reused without explicit permission from the creator.

Creative Commons

Can be reused with proper attribution. Always credit the original creator.

Fair Use

Limited use for commentary, criticism, or education may be permitted under fair use.

Music & Audio

Always check music licenses separately. Use YouTube's Audio Library for safe options.

Get Permission

When in doubt, contact the creator for written permission before using content.

Consequences

Copyright violations can lead to content removal, channel strikes, or legal action.
